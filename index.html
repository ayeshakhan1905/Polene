Meet

beri

Obtained by folding four pieces of leather and forming the handle, the “Béri” model exudes a strong yet sophisticated style with its tailored base and central clasp. In the Onda edition, it is adorned with a wavy leather relief. Each individual buckle is hand-placed and then stitched, highlighting the dexterity of the leather goods artisans.
